<h1>How to Embed a YouTube Video Into a Website</h1>

<h2>Step 1: Find a Video</h2>
<p>Open YouTube and search for the video you want to embed. Click on the video to open it.</p>
<img src="images/step1.png" alt="Step 1 screenshot">

<h2>Step 2: Click the Share Button</h2>
<p>Below the video, click the Share button to open more options.</p>
<img src="images/step2.png" alt="Step 2 screenshot">

<h2>Step 3: Select Embed</h2>
<p>In the pop-up window, click the Embed option.</p>
<img src="images/step3.png" alt="Step 3 screenshot">

<h2>Step 4: Copy the Embed Code</h2>
<p>Click the Copy button to copy the HTML embed code to your clipboard.</p>
<img src="images/step4.png" alt="Step 4 screenshot">

<h2>Step 5: Paste the Embed Code</h2>
<p>Paste the embed code into the body of your HTML page where you want the video to appear.</p>
<img src="images/step5.png" alt="Step 5 screenshot">
